About Us

Mission

Empowering Assam’s agricultural communities through sustainable farming, education, and innovation, blending traditional wisdom with modern techniques.

Vision

To become a leading agro-based institution in Northeast India where every initiative uplifts rural livelihoods and every farmer becomes a changemaker.

Founder — Kamal Uddin Choudhury

Kamal Uddin Choudhury is the founding pillar of Shouq Agro Estates, a visionary leader and social activist whose life is dedicated to Assam’s rural revival. With decades of experience as an NSS commander, educationalist, and agriculturalist, Kamal has transformed countless villages through youth mobilization, sustainable farming projects, and education initiatives. He believes that agriculture is not just about crops, but about cultivating communities, values, and futures. His guidance and mentorship have created a strong foundation for Shouq Agro Estates, turning it into a movement that empowers farmers and celebrates Assam’s agricultural heritage.

Director — Mustafijur Alom Choudhary (Moon Choudhary)

Moon Choudhary, the dynamic Director of Shouq Agro Estates, combines cutting-edge technical knowledge with a deep passion for community development. A proud NIT graduate and accomplished social activist, he spearheads innovative agricultural solutions while ensuring every program benefits rural communities. Moon’s vision ensures that Shouq Agro Estates stays at the forefront of agro-innovation, integrating technology with tradition, modern farming methods with Assam’s rich heritage, and empowering farmers to thrive sustainably in today’s world.

Our Subsidiaries

Shouq Dairy Farms

Ethical dairy farming with indigenous breeds and organic feed, delivering pure milk from pasture to people.

Shouq Nurseries

Cultivating high-quality saplings: fruit trees, medicinal plants, and native species for farmers & community gardens.

Shouq Livestock

Responsible livestock care with traditional knowledge & modern hygiene, focusing on animal health and rural employment.

Shouq Aqua Farms

Sustainable freshwater fish farming, preserving native species and generating rural livelihoods.

Shouq Organics

Organic vegetables, grains & herbs grown with natural composting and traditional techniques.

Shouq AgroTech

Smart farming solutions including soil testing, irrigation systems, and agri-data tools empowering farmers.
